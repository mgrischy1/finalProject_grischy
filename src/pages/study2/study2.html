Ionic forms are mostly used for interaction with users and collecting needed info. The best way to use forms is to use list and item as your main classes. Your app will usually consist more than one-form element, so it makes sense to organize it as a list. In the following example, you can notice that the item element is a label tag. You can use any other element, but a label will provide the ability to tap on any part of the element to focus your text input.
